Explanation of the Structure:
<header>: Contains the main heading of the webpage, typically representing the website title or logo.

<nav>: Includes the navigation links to different sections of the website, enhancing usability.

<main>: Serves as the primary content area of the webpage, containing all the main information.

<article>: Each article represents a separate piece of content within the main area. This is useful for blog posts, news articles, or any independent content.

<footer>: Contains footer information such as copyright notices or additional links.

Styling:
Basic CSS styles are included for layout and aesthetics, making the structure clear and user-friendly.
